I am Adam, of Eternia - VISITORS LOUNGE

Design Concept

For this visualizer, I wanted to create something vaporwave-like again, but this time with the popular statue head motif. The colors I used are to evoke the somewhat tacky pink-purple and blue look of vaporwave. To achieve the retro VHS look, I added a play button as well.

Software Used

The software I used to achieve this look is Cinema 4D, Trapcode, and Adobe After Effects.

Process

I first downloaded a free model of a greek statue bust, then changed the orientation in Cinema 4D and imported it into After Effects. I then used Trapcode Form to overlay a displacement layer on top of the head so it would look 3D but distorted by a grid of pink-purple lines. I then added a VHS static layer for a more 80s feels as well as an overlay of a play button. I then rendered out one full spin of the head then exported it to Adobe Premiere and timed it to the music so it would loop perfectly on Instagram.
